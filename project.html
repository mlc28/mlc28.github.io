2.2 MPG and a Type of Cars

The following boxplot shows how the distribution of highway MPG (hwy) varies by a type of cars (class) üöô üöö üöê.

ggplot(data = mpg) +
  geom_boxplot(aes(x = class, y = hwy, fill = class),
               show.legend = F) +
  labs(x = "Class", y = "Highway\nMPG")
